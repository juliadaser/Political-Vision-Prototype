News Week

Newsweek.com had an estimated 14.4 million monthly visits, with an average visit duration of 2 minutes and 20 seconds, and highest visit count from the United States and United Kingdom.

  • 65% of Newsweek visitors are male and 35% are female.
  • Most Newsweek readers are aged between 18 and 44, with 24% of visitors being 18-24,
    25% being 25-34, 20% being 35-44.
  • Newsweek has an approximately equal count of readers identifying as Democrats
    or Republicans, with 24% of all readers identifying as independents.
